How to represent Time?
1.	Minutes/Quarter/Half (To/Past)  Hour
      Example:
      five past three (3.05)

2.	Hour : Minute
      a)	Hour Minute (am or pm)
      Example:
      Three ten
      3:10
      4:05 pm

3.	Hour
      a)	Hour am or pm
      b)	Hour o’clock
      Example:
      6 am
      6 o’clock

4.	Key word
      a)	Midnight (0:00)
      b)	Noon / Midday (12:00)
